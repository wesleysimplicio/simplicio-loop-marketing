# HYPOTHESIS — Asolaria on Metal

## Core belief

If Simplicio is presented as an operator-first system that reduces complexity into bounded artifacts, technical audiences will understand it faster than if it is described as a vague "AI marketing platform".

## Why this fits Asolaria

- "map of maps" becomes a canonical index of the repo's own maps
- "reductions in everything" becomes a catalog of repo-local reductions
- "on metal" becomes a narrative about local, inspectable, operator-visible execution rather than a cloud-only black box

## Falsifiable questions

1. Do maintainers reach the relevant canonical docs faster?
2. Can a campaign asset be reviewed without opening engine core code?
3. Can a skeptical reader trace each headline claim to a spec, test, or artifact?

## Required proof before public promotion

- a real landing host
- a published demo artifact
- public analytics or benchmark receipts
- human-approved copy for every paid surface
