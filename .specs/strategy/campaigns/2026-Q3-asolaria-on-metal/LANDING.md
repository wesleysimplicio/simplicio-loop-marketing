## Problem

Most AI-marketing tooling asks operators to trust a black box:

- vague prompts
- hidden provider coupling
- unclear evidence
- public claims that outrun the artifacts

## Promise

Simplicio's marketing engine takes a stricter path:

- routing is documented
- artifacts are local and inspectable
- campaign planning is bounded
- promotion is gated
- lessons persist across iterations
